FAQ


1. What is Fabstitch?

Fabstitch provides online tailoring services at your door step. We help you to get your dress material stitched as per your fit and style. You can schedule a free pickup through fabstitch.in or from whatsapp. Our expert will visit your house to take your measurement and collect the dress material. We also offers services to choose from a wide range of dress materials and accessories for your outfit. It will take 7 working days to stitch, finish and deliver the order.

2. Why Fabstitch?

Still struggling with the tailoring experience!! Feeling lazy to visit a boutique!! Just go to fabstitch.in and select your free pickup and delivery times online and leave the rest with us. If you have difficulty placing your order, just call us. We will help you place your order.

Currently, we are offering stitching services for salwar suits, blouse, kurti, designer lahengas, designer salwar suits , kids frocks, men kurtas.

Yes. We provide free measurement, pickup and delivery for you as per your convenient chosen time. Our professional tailor will come at your doorstep on your scheduled pickup time to measure you and collect the dress material so that we can stitch it as per your measurements.
